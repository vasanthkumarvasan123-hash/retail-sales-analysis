# Retail Sales End-to-End Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales transactions to understand sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product category trends, payment preferences, and country-wise business performance.

The project was completed using Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights and interactive dashboards.

---

## Dataset Information

- Total Orders: 46,000+
- Total Sales: 51 Million
- Total Revenue: 38 Million
- Total Quantity Sold: 1 Million+
- Average Order Value: 1121

---

## Key Findings

- Generated 51 Million total sales across 46,000+ orders.
- Revenue reached 38 Million during the analysis period.
- Product categories contributed almost equally to overall sales volume.
- Germany, Belgium, United Kingdom, United States, and France were among the top-performing countries.
- Bank Transfer, PayPal, and Credit Card generated nearly equal sales contributions.
- Monthly sales remained relatively stable throughout the year with minor seasonal fluctuations.
- Customer purchasing activity was distributed across multiple product categories.
- Return trends remained consistent across different months.
